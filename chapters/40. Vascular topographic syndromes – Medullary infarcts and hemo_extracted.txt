L-Question: Patient presents with ipsilateral trigeminal sensory changes, contralateral limb/body sensory changes. Where is the lesion located?

S-Question: Patient presents with ipsilateral trigeminal sensory changes, contralateral limb/body sensory changes. What is the implicated artery?
S-Answer: occlusion of penetrating branches associated with distal VA stenoocclusive disease... Intrinsic PICA disease
